Featured Projects


Airtel Suvidha

  • Predictive analysis, Recommendation engine

Team Ignited_Minds presented a Recommendation Engine and Data analysis tool named Suvidha at the Airtel Whack Hackathon.
It studies user’s usage patterns and recommends optimal plans based on the pattern.
It also provides Airtel an understanding about how a package is performing and how it can be improved.

Contract Reader Tool

  • Automation, Natural Language Processing

We developed a contract reader tool bot at the EY Intelligent Automation Hackathon that performs contract completeness check, extraction of relevant clauses, performs applicability checks and subsequently shares the result report.

Featured Events

StreetHack Hackathon

Investopad, Gurgaon

StreetHack organized it’s flagship event Streethack 1.0 sponsored by CodeStreet & Github. This year we received an overwhelming response from professionals, startups and students as well. We called up 30 teams from 400 registrants to participate, develop a prototype and present their ideas to ESA, Talsinki Project, Finnish Forestry centre among others. Team GroundAssist won a trip to Finland to present their idea at Ultrahack Sprint I.

Check it out

Uhack Hackathon

USICT, GGS Indraprastha University, Delhi

A 2 day hackathon, organised at USICT, sponsored by CodeStreet and Coding Blocks saw participation from all over India. Problem statements was given by European Space Agency, Orion, SignSpace among others. More than 600 students joined as 200 teams and competed for 30 spots. 11 Team were shortlisted out of which one went to win a trip to Finland to present their idea & prototype at Ultrahack Sprint II and Slush as well.
